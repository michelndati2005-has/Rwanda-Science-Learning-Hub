About Our School

A Legacy of Faith & Excellence

Saint François de Sales School is a distinguished Catholic primary school dedicated to the holistic development of every child. Founded on the teachings of Saint François de Sales — the patron saint of writers, educators and the deaf — our school fosters intellectual curiosity, spiritual growth and moral integrity.

📍 Our Location

🏆 Our Mission

To provide an excellent Catholic education that forms the whole person — intellectually, spiritually, morally and socially — so that each student becomes a responsible and compassionate citizen.

Our Pillars

School Values & Services

Everything we do is guided by our core Catholic values and commitment to excellence.

✝
Faith & Spirituality
Rooted in Catholic tradition, we nurture every child's spiritual life through prayer, liturgy and sacraments.
📚
Academic Excellence
High standards of learning with dedicated teachers, structured curriculum and modern teaching methods.
🤝
Discipline & Respect
A safe, orderly and respectful environment where every student is valued and heard.
🎨
Creative Arts
Music, art, drama and creative writing programs that develop expression and talent.
⚽
Sports & Health
Physical education, team sports and wellness activities for healthy body and mind.
🌍
Community Service
Instilling a sense of social responsibility through outreach programs and community projects.
